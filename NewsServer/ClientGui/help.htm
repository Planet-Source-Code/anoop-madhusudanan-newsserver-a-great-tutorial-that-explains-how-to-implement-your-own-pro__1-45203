Help
Welcome to the I-News Client. This program will help you read news in an I-News server.
How to read news?
To connect to a server, you can click either the Signup button, or the Login button. Once you signed up or logged in, you will see a list of available categories and news in the server. Double click a news item to read it. You can click the refresh button any time, to refresh the categories and news
How to Disconnect?
Click the Logout button to disconnect. You will be disconnected automatically if an error occurs.
